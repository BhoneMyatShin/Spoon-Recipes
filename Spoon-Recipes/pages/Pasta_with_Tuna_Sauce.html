Pasta with Tuna Sauce

This easy pasta with tuna and tomato sauce is made with canned tuna and any type of pasta.

Recipe list

  • 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 medium onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves crushed garlic

  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can crushed tomatoes

  • 1 tablespoon capers

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

  • ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or to taste

  • 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained

Steps

  • Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.

  • Heat oil over low heat in a large skillet. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, capers, lemon juice, parsley, and pepper flakes. Simmer gently until sauce thickens, about 3 minutes. Fold in tuna and cook until heated through.

  • Drain penne and return to the pot. Pour sauce over pasta and toss to coat.
